Smollan Family Tree

An interactive family tree tracing the descendants of Zelig (David) Smollan of Suwalki, Russia — spanning seven generations from the 1860s through to the early 2000s, and across three continents.

→ View the family tree


How to use it


Click any name to expand or collapse that branch of the family
Click again on a name to see full details — birth dates, marriage information, locations, and notes
Search by name using the search bar at the top to jump straight to a person
Use Expand all to see the entire tree at once, or Collapse all to start fresh



Coverage

The tree covers 7 generations descending from Zelig (David) Smollan, including branches that settled in:


England (Middlesbrough, London, Sheffield, Sunderland)
South Africa (Johannesburg, Port Elizabeth)
Israel (including families who made Aliyah from the 1940s onward)
Australia (Melbourne, Hobart)
New Zealand
Canada (Toronto, Scarborough)
United States (Philadelphia, Los Angeles, Miami, Florida)
